Exploring My Passions in Computer Engineering

11 July 2025

My computer engineering journey has sparked several strong interests. Databases immediately clicked with me;
I found the logic intuitive and excelled in the subject. Networking also piques my curiosity, fascinated by how devices communicate and the internet's structure.
Beyond software, I'm deeply interested in CPU technology and wafer fabrication, thanks to my Analog and Computer Architecture courses. Learning about the physical creation of these powerful chips is truly captivating.
Finally, I've begun self-studying Quantum Computing. While incredibly challenging and complex, its revolutionary potential is highly intriguing, and I'm committed to exploring it further at my own pace.
